poly-zoom

Bring an incredible zoom (60fps) to your project using this element

Usage:

  • upper-limit is the maximum zoom posible
  • revere is the direction of the zoom with the mouse wheel
<poly-zoom upper-limit="3" reverse="true">
  <!-- everything here -->
  <img>
  <h1>Hello!</h1>
</poly-zoom>
